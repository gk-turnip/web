Game Design Document

Overview

Gourdian Knot is a game about a world called the Knot, populated by player characters called Whatknots. The Knot was created by seventeen legendary Gourds, who watch over it to this day.

Basic Gameplay

The game is open-ended and sandboxlike, centered around interacting with objects, other users, and the environment through chat, crafting, building, trading, and exploration.

There is also a strong community component. Communal efforts are encouraged and rewarded both by game mechanics and by the goodwill of other players.

Game Geography World View

The Knot is composed of many individual pods, each belonging to a linear string; the cross-connections between different strings form the tangle that is the Knot.

Game Geography Detail View

An individual pod is a closed, self-sufficient environment with a number of entrances and exits. Pods will contain some combination of resources to be harvested, NPCs or game-provided landmarks, and player-constructed buildings.
